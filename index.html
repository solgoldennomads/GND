Degen Coin Dozer - GND

Introducing Degen Coin Dozer - GND, an innovative smart contract-based coin pusher game resembling an arcade machine. Using Solana, players push coins and prizes on the edge, aiming to strategically time their coin drops for a cascade effect and win Solana by pushing valuable items over the edge. With a blend of chance and skill, it offers an exhilarating and captivating experience for players of all ages. Diverse game modes and machines provide added excitement, while the transparency of on-chain transaction tracking allows for audits by anyone, ensuring a trustworthy and enjoyable gaming environment.
